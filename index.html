No more monthly processing fee debits

By using net deposits we are able to remove the payment processing fees from each transaction automatically, prior to depositing the transaction to your program’s bank account. This means that the money deposited into your bank account is your program’s money and the only thing that will be debited are your monthly software fees.

Apple Pay / Google Pay / Samsung Pay

We now fully support these one-touch payment methods which provide frictionless card payments and eliminate the need to manually type card details, saving time at registration for you and your community. If your program wants these payment options enabled contact our Support Team.

Instant verification of bank accounts

Families can now instantly verify their ACH information by connecting their checking or savings account using their bank credentials.

Improved security and reliability

Along with enhanced security, our payment processing systems will now have 99.999% uptime.

PCI compliance

Our integrated payment processor has been audited by a PCI-certified auditor and is certified to PCI Service Provider Level 1. This is the most stringent level of certification available.

Future product enhancements

By making this change we have the option to explore future payment-processing enhancements such as pay in person, buy-now pay-later, crypto, and more.
